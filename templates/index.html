How the 3-Layer System Works

From your daily inputs to actionable maintenance intelligence

LAYER 1
📥

Raw Layer

You log daily data: usage hours, load %, fuel consumed, faults, NEPA outage hours. Simple daily form entry runs in under 60 seconds.

• Daily usage hours (0–24)
• Load level % of KVA capacity
• Fuel consumed (litres)
• Fault type & count
• NEPA/PHCN outage hours
LAYER 2
⚙️

Processing Layer

Raw inputs are aggregated and transformed into 5 scientifically-derived health indicators — each measuring a different failure dimension.

• UII — Usage Intensity Index
• FER — Fuel Efficiency Ratio
• MOS — Maintenance Overdue Score
• AALS — Age-Adjusted Load Stress
• FFR — Fault Frequency Rate
LAYER 3
🔮

Prediction Layer

A weighted scoring algorithm produces a Composite Health Score (0–100) with risk classification and prioritised maintenance actions.

🟢 HEALTHY  Score 75–100
🟡 MODERATE  Score 50–75
🟠 HIGH RISK  Score 25–50
🔴 CRITICAL  Score 0–25
